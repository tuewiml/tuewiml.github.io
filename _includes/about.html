About Women In Machine Learning (WiML) Tübingen

WiML Tübingen is a group of women pushing the boundries of societal stereotypes and working to advance the inclusion of women in AI.

We are fueled by a passion to cause positive impact in the society through machine learning.

We specialize in natural language processing, and computer vision, aiming to solve complex problems and drive progress.

Education and community engagement are central to our mission, as we strive to empower the next generation of AI experts through workshops and mentorship.
